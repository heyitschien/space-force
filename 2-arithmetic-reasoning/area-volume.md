# 🎯 ASVAB LESSON: AREA & VOLUME (ARITHMETIC REASONING)

# 🔵 PART 1 — AREA (2D SPACE)

## 🔷 Principle 1: Rectangle Area

📌 Formula:
[
\text{Area} = \text{length} \times \text{width}
]

### 🧠 Meaning:

You’re counting **how many squares fit inside**

---

### ✅ Example (From your test)

> Carpet a 12 ft × 12 ft room 

[
A = 12 \times 12 = 144 \text{ ft}^2
]

✔️ Answer: **144**

---

### 🧪 Practice

1. A room is 10 ft × 15 ft. Area = ?
2. A field is 20 m × 30 m. Area = ?

---

## 🔷 Principle 2: Square

📌 Formula:
[
A = s^2
]

### Example

Side = 9

[
A = 9^2 = 81
]

---

### 🧪 Practice

1. Side = 7 → Area = ?
2. Side = 12 → Area = ?

---

## 🔷 Principle 3: Triangle

📌 Formula:
[
A = \frac{1}{2} \times base \times height
]

### Example

Base = 10, height = 6

[
A = \frac{1}{2}(10)(6) = 30
]

---

### 🧪 Practice

1. Base 8, height 5 → Area = ?
2. Base 12, height 4 → Area = ?

---

## 🔷 Principle 4: Circle

📌 Formula:
[
A = \pi r^2
]

(Use π ≈ 3.14 unless told otherwise)

---

### Example

Radius = 3

[
A = 3.14 \times 9 = 28.26
]

---

### 🧪 Practice

1. Radius = 5 → Area = ?
2. Diameter = 10 → Area = ?

---

# 🔵 PART 2 — UNIT TRAPS (VERY IMPORTANT ⚠️)

ASVAB LOVES THIS.

## 🔷 Key Conversion

[
1 \text{ yard} = 3 \text{ feet}
]

[
1 \text{ square yard} = 9 \text{ square feet}
]

---

### 🔥 Example (From your test)

> 16 ft × 18 ft carpet, cost per **square yard** 

Step 1: Area in ft²
[
16 \times 18 = 288
]

Step 2: Convert to yd²
[
288 ÷ 9 = 32
]

---

### 🧪 Practice

1. 180 ft² → yd² = ?
2. 90 ft² → yd² = ?

# 🔵 PART 3 — VOLUME (3D SPACE)

## 🔷 Principle 5: Rectangular Prism

📌 Formula:
[
V = l \times w \times h
]

---

### Example

Box: 4 × 3 × 2

[
V = 24
]

---

### 🧪 Practice

2. 10 × 2 × 6 → Volume = ?

]

---

👉 Area = **covering**
👉 Volume = **filling**

👉 Always ask:
