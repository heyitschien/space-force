# ⚡ MASTER FORMULA (LOCK THIS IN)

## 🧩 The 2 Equations You Actually Need

**1. Normal Average**

```
Average = Total ÷ Count
```

**2. ASVAB Power Move (MOST IMPORTANT)**

```
Total = Average × Count
```

👉 This is the one that wins on the test.

## 🔥 THE FAST METHOD (NO CONFUSION)

### Formula:

```
Missing Value = (Target Average × Total Count) − Current Sum
```

---

## 🧠 THINK LIKE THIS:

👉 “What total do I NEED?”
👉 “What total do I HAVE?”
👉 “What’s missing?”

---

# ⚡ STEP-BY-STEP SYSTEM

### Step 1 — Add what you have

### Step 2 — Find target total

### Step 3 — Subtract

---

# 💥 EXAMPLE (YOUR STYLE)

Scores: 85, 90, 88
Target average: 88
Total tests: 4

---

### Step 1 — Current sum

85 + 90 + 88 = **263**

---

### Step 2 — Target total

88 × 4 = **352**

---

### Step 3 — Missing score

352 − 263 = **89**

✅ Answer: **89**

---

# 🧠 5-YEAR-OLD VERSION

👉 You need **352 total points**
👉 You already have **263 points**

So…

👉 “How much more do I need?”

👉 352 − 263 = **89**
