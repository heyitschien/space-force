Concepts: percent of a number, percent increase/decrease, tax, tips, discounts, “X is Y% of Z”
Examples: 12% of $375; 7% raise; 10% off one item; 20% then 15% off; 40/60 to pass

Unit conversion (10 questions)
Rules: 1 gross = 144; 1 ton = 2,000 lb; 1 lb = 16 oz; 1 yd = 3 ft; 1 yd² = 9 ft²; 1 km ≈ 5/8 mile; 1 dozen = 12; 1 gal = 4 qt
Concepts: converting units in multi-step problems
Examples: 144 ÷ 3 bottles/day; 3 tons → oz → residents; feet/inches to board length
Practice IDs: ar-p1-003, ar-p1-018, ar-p1-025, ar-p1-027; ar-p2-011, ar-p2-022
Rate–distance–time (9 questions)
Formulas: distance = speed × time; time = distance ÷ speed; speed = distance ÷ time
Concepts: catch-up (Alice/Dave), trains moving apart, gas cost from distance
Examples: 1,650 ÷ 50 mph; 55×3 + 70×3; 48×7 miles ÷ 21 mpg × $2.82/gal
Practice IDs: ar-p1-010, ar-p1-013, ar-p1-023, ar-p1-026, ar-p1-030; ar-p2-027 (Pythagorean)
Fractions (8 questions)
Rules: LCD for add/subtract; fraction × whole; “X is 2/5 of Y” → Y = X ÷ (2/5)
Concepts: fraction of a quantity, fraction of time, “one-fifth more” = 1.2×
Examples: 1/4 + 1/3 + 1/6 of 20 pies; 2/5 × 40 hours; 600,000 = 1.2x
Practice IDs: ar-p1-011, ar-p1-017, ar-p1-029; ar-p2-003, ar-p2-017, ar-p2-024
Word-problem setup (6 questions)
Concepts: translating words to equations; “sum of two numbers,” “X more than Y,” remainder/division
Examples: x + (x+8) = 70; 47 ÷ 6 = 7 r5; 104 ÷ 42 trips; parallelogram angles
Practice IDs: ar-p1-002; ar-p2-013, ar-p2-015, ar-p2-021, ar-p2-026
Averages (5 questions)
Formulas: mean = sum ÷ count; (sum + x) ÷ (n+1) = target → solve for x
Concepts: finding missing value to hit target average
Examples: (93+89+96+98+x) ÷ 5 = 95; (92+88+86+x) ÷ 4 = 90
Practice IDs: ar-p1-004, ar-p1-022; ar-p2-005, ar-p2-016, ar-p2-023
