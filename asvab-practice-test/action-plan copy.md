# ASVAB 8-Week Action Calendar (V2 Gap-Closure Plan)

This calendar is aligned to `important-all-categories-review-concepts.md` (V2) and designed for high confidence, high retention, and steady score growth over 56 days.

## Rules For Every Study Day

- [ ] Total time: 60 minutes minimum (90 minutes on long days)
- [ ] Daily math touch (20 to 30 min): AR or MK every day
- [ ] Timed drill (10 to 20 min) + review of mistakes (10 to 15 min)
- [ ] End with 5 "confidence wins" (easy/medium questions you can solve cleanly)
- [ ] Log every miss as one of: `concept`, `setup`, `careless`, `time`

## Daily Session Format (Default 60 Minutes)

- [ ] 5 min warm-up: formula and vocabulary quick recall
- [ ] 25 min deep block: daily primary topic
- [ ] 15 min timed set: speed and pressure adaptation
- [ ] 10 min error log correction: redo every miss without notes
- [ ] 5 min confidence close: 5 easier reps
