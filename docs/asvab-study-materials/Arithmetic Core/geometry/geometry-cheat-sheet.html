📐 ASVAB Geometry Master Cheat Sheet

Identity Shape → Pick Formula → Plug & Chug

🟦 RECTANGLE

Length (L) Width (W)
  • Perimeter: P = 2L + 2W
  • Area: A = L × W
  • ⚠️ TRAP: Area units are always squared (in², ft²).

🔺 TRIANGLE

  • Perimeter: P = s1 + s2 + s3
  • Area: A = ½ × b × h
  • ⚠️ TRAP: Height (h) must be perpendicular (straight up)!

⚪ CIRCLE

r
  • Circumference: C = 2πr or πd
  • Area: A = πr²
  • ⚠️ TRAP: Diameter (d) = 2 × radius. Divide by 2 first!

🧠 ASVAB RECOGNITION DRILL

  • "Fencing a yard" → Perimeter
  • "Installing carpet/grass" → Area
  • "A revolving wheel/tire" → Circumference
  • "Space inside a box/tank" → Volume

🚨 THE "TRAP" CHECKLIST

  • Did I use the radius (r) or diameter (d)?
  • Did I remember the ½ for the triangle?
  • Are all units the same (inches vs feet) before I started?
  • Does the answer need π or 3.14?

🧪 QUICK MENTAL CHECK:

Rectangle: L=10, W=5 → Area is 50, Perimeter is 30.

Triangle: Base=6, Height=4 → Area is 12 (Don't forget the half!).
