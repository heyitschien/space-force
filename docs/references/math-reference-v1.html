Verified Formulas & Recognition Drills

2D Shapes & Circles

Rectangle

Perimeter: P = 2l + 2w

Area: A = lw

*Add all sides for any polygon perimeter.

Triangle

Area: A = ½bh

Sum of Angles: 180°

Pythagorean: a² + b² = c² (Right Δ)

Circle

Circumference: C = 2πr OR πd

Area: A = πr²

*Use π ≈ 3.14 or 22/7.

3D Volume & Surface Area

Cube: V = a³ | SA = 6a²
Cylinder: V = πr²h
Rect. Pyramid: V = ⅓abh
Sphere: V = 4/3πr³

Linear Equations

  • Slope (m): (y₂ - y₁) / (x₂ - x₁)
  • Slope-Intercept: y = mx + b
  • Point-Slope: y - y₁ = m(x - x₁)
  • Distance: d = √[(x₂-x₁)² + (y₂-y₁)²]

Exponents & Roots

a⁰ = 1
a⁻ⁿ = 1/aⁿ
(aᵐ)ⁿ = aᵐⁿ
aᵐ · aⁿ = aᵐ⁺ⁿ

Statistics

Mean: Sum of points / Number of points

Median: Middle value (ordered small to large)

Mode: Occurs most often

Range: Max - Min

Interest & Percent

Simple Interest: I = prt

Percent Change: [(New - Old) / Old] × 100

Probability: Desired outcomes / Total outcomes

Linear

3 ft = 1 yd

5,280 ft = 1 mi

Capacity

8 fl oz = 1 cup

2 cups = 1 pt

2 pt = 1 qt

4 qt = 1 gal

Weight & Time

1 lb = 16 oz

1 Ton = 2,000 lbs

1 hr = 3,600 sec
