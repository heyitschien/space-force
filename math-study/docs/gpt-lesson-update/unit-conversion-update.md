# 🔷 LEVEL 1 — CORE PRINCIPLE (THIS IS EVERYTHING)

## 🔥 The Golden Rule

> **Conversions are just multiplying by 1**

Example:
[
\frac{1 \text{ gallon}}{4 \text{ quarts}} = 1
]

So:
[
5 \text{ gal} \times \frac{4 \text{ qt}}{1 \text{ gal}} = 20 \text{ qt}
]

👉 Units cancel like algebra.

---

## 🔥 The Unit Cancellation Law

If units don’t cancel → **you did it wrong**
